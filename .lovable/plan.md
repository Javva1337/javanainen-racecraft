
# Rickard Javanainen – Premium Racing Website

## Design System
- **Dark theme**: Deep blacks (#0a0a0a, #141414), dark grays (#1a1a1a, #262626)
- **Accent**: Warm gold (#c9a96e) for CTAs, highlights, and borders
- **Typography**: Clean, modern sans-serif with generous letter-spacing for headings
- **Animations**: Subtle fade-in on scroll, smooth transitions on hover

## Pages & Sections (Single-page layout)

### 1. Navigation
- Sticky top bar, transparent → solid on scroll
- Links: Hem, Om, Karriär, Resultat, Galleri, Partners, Kontakt
- Gold accent on active/hover states

### 2. Hero (Fullscreen)
- Dark cinematic background with gradient overlay
- "Rickard Javanainen" as large heading
- Subtitle: "VM-satsande racingförare | Strategisk racecraft | Bevisad prestation"
- Two buttons: "Se karriär" / "Bli partner"
- Subtle scroll indicator at bottom

### 3. Positionering
- Three columns with key stats: "Top 3 i VM", "Strategisk racecraft", "VM Billund 2025"
- Short, confident copy building credibility
- Gold accent lines/icons

### 4. Om Rickard
- Brief bio text, professional tone
- Portrait placeholder area
- Focus on elite mindset and strategic approach

### 5. Karriär (Timeline)
- Vertical timeline with milestones
- Gold dots/lines connecting entries
- Years, events, achievements with fade-in animation

### 6. Resultat
- Clean table/card layout showing race results
- Podium placements highlighted with gold
- Columns: Tävling, Plats, Datum, Position

### 7. Galleri
- Grid of image placeholders with hover zoom effect
- Lightbox modal on click
- Dark borders, premium feel

### 8. Sponsorer / Partners
- Value proposition: Exponering, Varumärke, Representation
- Grid for sponsor logo placeholders
- Prominent gold "Bli partner" CTA button

### 9. Kontakt
- Simple form: Namn, E-post, Meddelande
- Email display
- Clean, minimal layout

### 10. Footer
- Minimal footer with copyright and social links
